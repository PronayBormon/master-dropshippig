Easily manage your online store in BIGC

  • Store management and partners

    You train your subordinates to open stores, and you will receive the profits of your subordinate stores and the company's monthly salary. In the process of inviting new members, you will receive store opening subsidies, bonuses and store service discounts.

  • Shops and products

    The system automatically creates a store and adds products based on your store criteria. You need to replenish operating funds reasonably to maintain the good operating status of the store. The company will add brand products from time to time according to market demand to ensure that store users have a rich variety of products.

  • Order management and distribution

    Please process orders in a timely manner every day. Uncompleted orders can be transferred to cooperative stores for processing. No need to worry about shipping speed and shipping risks. We will deliver your order to the brand warehouse as quickly as possible and track the dynamic information of the goods transportation until the user signs and settles the transaction.
